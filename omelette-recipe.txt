Ingredients:
 - 1 large egg
 - 1/4 cup butter
 - 1/4 teaspoon salt
 - 1/2 tomato
 - 1/2 onion
 - 3 green chilis

Directions:
  1. Finely dice the tomato, onion and chilis
  2. In a medium saucepan, melt half of the butter
  3. Fry the diced vegetables on medium heat for about a minute
  4. Crack and beat the egg along with the fried veggies and salt
  5. In the previous saucepan, melt the remaining butter
  6. Pour in the egg-veggies-salt mixture and fry on high heat until fluffy
  7. Bon appetit!
